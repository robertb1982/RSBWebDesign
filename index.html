UI/UX Designer / Front-End Developer

Hi, I'm Rob! I'm a UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer from Boston. Currently working at Rockland Trust as a User Experience Designer, and previously worked at AAA Northeast as a UI/UX Designer & Developer.

Why RSB Web Design?

The purpose of RSB Web Design is to be able to showcase my skills, web projects (both freelance and work-related), and overall progress as I thrive to become a better designer and developer. Even this website is a work in progress, but this is a journey I am very passionate about.

I like to divide my web projects into four areas: comprehensive research, wireframing, design and development. This process allows me to craft beautiful user interfaces and design amazing user experiences that will reach goals and make a difference at all levels.

When I'm not focusing on design and development, I enjoy jamming out on my guitars while collaborating with musicians on new projects.

Recent Work: Fischer Gourmet

For Fischer Gourmet, I designed and developed a website that would render a great user experience across all device types. I was responsible for UI design, interactions & front-end development.

To design the new website for Fischer Gourmet, I listened to Private Chef Dan Fischer's vision and incorporated his core design elements.
